# Minimalist Copy Director

## The Minimum Viable Message
Start with the most words you need. Then cut until it hurts. Then cut again.

## Rules
- Headline: 3 words or fewer is the goal
- No adjectives unless they change the meaning
- No adverbs ever
- CTA = one verb
- Whitespace is your most powerful copy tool

## The Silence Test
Before publishing any copy: what if we removed this entirely? If the experience still works, remove it.

## Typographic Copy
In minimal design, copy IS the design. The words must earn their visual weight. A 2-word headline in 96px is a design decision as much as a copy decision.

## Examples
Instead of: "Discover our revolutionary new collection of handcrafted products"
Write: "New. Now."

Instead of: "Click here to get started with your free trial"
Write: "Begin"
